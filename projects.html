Final Project

Ideation

For this final project, I thought of something close to my heart and decided to use the skills I learnt to create a small ring box. My initial idea was to have a ring box that has mood lighting installed within it, with a rotating ring holder and have ambient music play when its open. I decided to call the project: "My Damn Extra Ring Box"

Arduino Circuit Component

This component was the worst to work on. It seriously took the longest time to work on - as of writing this, I am STILL working on the circuit, and I have spent at least 9 DAYS in a span of 4 WEEKS just figuring this part of the project out. I hardly understand circuits in its hardware form, much less the software coding. With the help of various classmates and lecturers and online resources, I managed to figure out a little something on Tinkercad. In my circuit, an LDR will serve as an input to command the Neopixel LED strip to on and the DC motor to rotate; my outputs. Since I was utilising an online library for my rainbow LEDs from an external source, I could not include that library simulation. Instead, I opted to programme the LDR and DC motor on tinkercad, then use the C++ code from there to implement into my actual circuit. Here is the online circuit and C++code.

Afterwards, with the use of an online Rainbow Neopixel LED library, I combined the code from the library with the code on tinkercad to programme my entire circuit. Attached is the circuit in real life, with the C++ code used:
