9 May 2022 to 11 Aug 2022 - Fusion 360

The first week was a rough one, we started using Fusion360 today and I had absolutely no prior experience using it. Fortunately, my skills of literally copying every single step my lecturer took was sufficient enough for me to create multiple objects in Fusion360. Definitely cool to see what Fusion360 is capable of.

In a span of weeks, We were tasked to create multiple models, including a wrench, some clock shaped thing, a lego brick, a mug, a hex nut, keychains, and a chess piece.

Each project started with a sketch with dimensions laid out for us, which I adapted into Fusion360 and with the use of various tools such as revovle, rectangular pattern, combine, cut etc, I managed to develop these few items.
